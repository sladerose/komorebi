THE PROPERTY

Komorebi is a comfortable ground floor self-catering apartment, less than 100 m from the beach and within walking distance to a supermarket and restaurants. This air-conditioned apartment can accommodate up to a maximum of 6 guests. The modern kitchen is fully equipped for self-catering. The lounge features a TV with premium DSTV and opens to a private patio with braai facilities. Safe and secure parking is available and accessed by remote control. The complex offers a swimming pool as well.
